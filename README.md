# 👋 Hello, I'm Alex

I am a **Software Engineering Student** and **Backend Developer** with a deep-seated passion for architecting systems that are not just functional, but truly efficient. I view software development as a craft where logic meets creativity—I don't just write code; I design solutions that help scale ideas and eliminate friction.

---

### 🚀 A Deeper Look into My Craft

* **Backend Architecture:** My core expertise lies in the **Java** ecosystem, specifically crafting microservices with **Spring Boot**. I also leverage **Python/Django** for rapid development and have recently been expanding my toolkit with **C#** for enterprise-level applications and robust backend logic.
* **The Power of Automation:** I firmly believe that if a task is repetitive, it shouldn't be manual. I specialize in **RPA (Robotic Process Automation)** using **UiPath**, and I frequently use **Puppeteer** for advanced web scraping and browser-based automation.
* **Modern Frontend & Mobile:** I build dynamic, high-performance web applications using **Next.js** and **React**. Additionally, I extend my development capabilities to mobile platforms with **React Native**, creating seamless cross-platform experiences.
* **Infrastructure & Linux:** I am highly comfortable working within **Linux** environments. Whether it's managing servers, optimizing workflows, or handling **Docker** containers, I thrive in the stability and flexibility of the open-source ecosystem.
* **Professional Tooling:** I am a power user of the **JetBrains** ecosystem. My workflow is deeply integrated with **IntelliJ IDEA**, **PyCharm**, and **WebStorm**, allowing me to maintain high code quality and efficient debugging across various languages and frameworks.
